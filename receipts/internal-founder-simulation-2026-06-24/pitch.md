LedgerOps AI helps finance support teams answer customer questions over ERP tickets, policy docs, and billing events.

Today support teams copy customer context across Zendesk, NetSuite, Stripe, and internal policy docs. Agents answer the easy questions but miss refund policy edge cases, invent billing details, and require senior finance operators to review every response. LedgerOps AI keeps the evidence inside the customer's tenant, cites the source record for every answer, and only proposes actions until a human approves them.

The first wedge is usage-based SaaS companies with high-volume billing tickets and strict refund policies. The activation event is a support lead connecting one ticket queue, running the eval set against ten historical tickets, and approving the first suggested reply inside one day. Retention is the second approved workflow: refund triage, billing correction, or contract-policy answer.

The platform risk is direct. Model providers can answer questions, but they do not own the customer's billing policy, refund history, approval workflow, or audit trail. The moat is the customer's private eval set plus the approved-action ledger that compounds with every resolved ticket.
